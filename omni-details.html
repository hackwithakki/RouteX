Maruti Suzuki Omni Overview

The Maruti Suzuki Omni is a practical and affordable van that has been one of the first high roof vehicles in India. Known for its versatility, the Omni has served as a people-mover, ambulance, and courier-delivery vehicle over its decades of production.

Verdict

The Omni was one of the first high roof vans in India to perform varied roles like a people-mover, ambulance, courier-delivery vehicle, and more. Now, 34 years on, it continues to be powered by a weak 35bhp petrol/CNG engine, has a four-speed gearbox and tiny wheels. It is a basic van that does practicality well but one that has no crash protection and is well past its prime. Production has been discontinued, but it remains an iconic vehicle in Indian automotive history.
